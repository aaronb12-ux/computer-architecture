**D Latch Behavior:**
- When the clock is 1 (high), the D latch is **transparent**: Q follows D
- When the clock is 0 (low), the D latch **holds** its state: Q keeps its old value
- Whenever D changes and the clock is 0, Q remains unchanged (nothing happens)
